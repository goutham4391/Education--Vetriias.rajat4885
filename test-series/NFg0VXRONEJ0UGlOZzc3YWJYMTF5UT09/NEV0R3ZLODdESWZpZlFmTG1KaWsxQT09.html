Course Structure

TNPSC GR-1 PRELIMS T.S.2

Structure of the Test Series

  • Test Series consists of 18 Tests
  •       Subject Based Test – 16
  •       Mock Test – 2
  • Batch – I: Wednesday & Saturday (2pm – 5pm)
  • Batch – II: Saturday & Sunday (2pm – 5pm)
  • Both Offline & Online Test Series available
  • Discussion after the completion of Test
  • Results will be declared on the same day

Features of the Programme

  • Provides an excellent & well-organized test batch which helps you to revise the syllabus in a well-structured manner
  • Each Test consists on Aptitude & Current Affairs
  • Well – Experienced subject experts who provide individual attention to everyone, which helps the candidates to qualify for the examination
  • Question papers will be prepared by a team of experienced Analysts & Reviewers
