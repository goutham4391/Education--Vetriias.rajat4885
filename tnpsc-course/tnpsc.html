Tamil Nadu Public Service Commission

The Combined Civil Services Examination is taken by Tamilnadu Public Service Commission to appoint candidates for different posts in the Tamilnadu State Administration. The Notification will be announced every year according to the annual planner which is released by the TNPSC. All the details of Vacancies, dates and registration process will be updated via notification on the official website only. There are thousands of Recruitments conducted every year for TNPSC.
