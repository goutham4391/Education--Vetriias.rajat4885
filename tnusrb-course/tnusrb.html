Tamil Nadu Uniformed Services Recruitment Board

Tamil Nadu Uniformed Services Recruitment Board (TNUSRB) conducts a state-level recruitment exam every year for the post of sub-inspector in Taluk, Armed Reserved and Tamilnadu Special Police in the state of Tamilnadu. The exam is conducted to recruit eligible fresh candidates and departmental candidates for the Tamil Nadu Police force. The latest announcements regarding the vacancies and the recruitment process will be updated in the official website of TNUSRB.
